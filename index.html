Parking Intelligence

Waikiki (Royal Hawaiian)

Park at Royal Hawaiian Ctr. Validated rate is cheapest. Buy a drink at the food court to validate.

Shark's Cove

Tiny lot. Arrive before 9:00 AM or park across the street near the food trucks (carefully).

Manoa Falls

Official lot is ~$7 (credit card kiosk). Do not park in residential areas; high ticket risk.

General Advice

Leave nothing visible in the Jeep. Leave doors unlocked if soft top (prevents slashing).
