My Notes for Us

Weather Wisdom

Jan rain = rainbows. Let's pack light rain jackets. Whale watching is free from the beach!

Driving

No honking, just aloha. We need to gas up early because stations are rare on the North Shore.

Vegan Spots

I've got the HappyCow app ready. I'll make sure we check for hidden ingredients.

Safety
